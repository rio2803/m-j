Instruction and Tips
  1. Go to mjaipur.com
  2. Place the cursor (Click mouse pointer) inside the first text box Just below Starting Jaipur Bus Stop.
  3. Source Location / Jaipur bus stops name Enter the first 2 or 3 (two or three) characters of name of the source location where you want to board the bus.
  4. Now select the source location from the list that appears just after you have typed a few characters.
  5. Destination Location / Jaipur bus stops name - Place the cursor (Click mouse pointer) inside the second text box just below Ending Jaipur Bus Stop or press TAB in your keyboard.
  6. Enter the first 2 or 3 (two or three) characters of name of the destination location where you want to board the bus.
  7. Now select the destination location from the list that appears just after you have typed a few characters.
  8. Now click on the Get Jaipur Bus Route button to search and display the details.
  9. The Fastest maximum available option you will be shown including distance between bus stops and Low floor bus route maps, Semi-low floor bus route maps, Private city mini bus route maps, Ac low floor Bus route maps, tempo map routes, Tata-Magic Map routes.

Jaipur Bus Route Time-Table and Schedule

Generally speking, the local private city mini bus route map direction are in operation from 5.00 AM (Morning) to 9.30 PM (Evening). Some suburban and urban jaipur city mini bus route may stops as early as around 8.00 PM.

Jaipur Bus Route Fare / Tickets :

  1. Jaipur bus Tickets can be bought from the ticket offices in private or public bus.
  2. If you change buses, you need to buy a new ticket for each journey in Jaipur City Bus.
  3. You cannot buy a "return" bus ticket in Jaipur city bus.
  4. A one-day bus pass allows you to make any number of low floor bus journeys during the same day.
  5. A monthly low floor bus pass allows you to make any number of low floor bus journeys during the month.

  7. For Adult, the daily bus fare will cost Rs 50, and the monthly pass Rs 700
  8. Night bus fares are the same as the fares for daytime buses.
